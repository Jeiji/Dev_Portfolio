Whym
at Sazze

Whym was a platform allowing spontaneous and whymsical travelers to find last-minute deals on tours, attractions, and other events in the location they are visiting. We marketed our brand and designed for the young savvy professional, but we even found a market amongst those in the more mature crowd.

State Insurance Pages
at GetInsured

Insurance is a very complex concept to grasp for most people. Allowing every-day people to get a good grasp of what they're investing in and what their specific state of residence's regulations entail was the primary goal for this project. I was tasked with brainstorming, ideating, wireframing, mocking, and finally building these pages out on my own--quite a valuable holistic experience for me.

Dishcrawl

If you're looking for a fun night out without knowing what on earth you'd like to eat, Dishcrawl would be the service for you. The whole idea is that you go out either alone or with friends, and embark on a food tour with other strangers. On this tour, you visit restaurants you may have never ventured to, and sample dishes you might not have tried otherwise. I designed most of the digital and web experience for those interested in trying it out.
